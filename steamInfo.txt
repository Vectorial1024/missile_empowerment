This mod increases the number of Missiles that the Albion Skunk can store from 150 to 240. (+90 capacity, an +60% increase)

Perhaps you started a fight with a full clip, but somehow used them all up in the middle of the fight, and got beaten up by enemy fighters. This mod mitigates that. By providing a larger Missile clip to Albion Skunk, you should be able to fight longer engagements. Also, you don't have to visit the Arms Dealers that often. Yay!

Incompatibilities
This is just a simple patch mod with one file and one patch opeartion. It should work with any mod out there. However, I am confident that this mod will not work with those mods that modify the player ship into something else.

Applicable Game Versions
Because of the simplicity of this mod, I boldly assume that this mod can work with [b]any game version[/b], from v1.00 up to v4.30.

Save-Game Safety
Can be removed anytime. Over-limit Missiles will not be removed, but then you won't be able to replenish the used over-limit Missiles.

May fortune favor you!
